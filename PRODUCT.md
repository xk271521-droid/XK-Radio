# Product

## Register

product

## Users

XK Radio is used by desktop music listeners who want a private, immersive player rather than a plain playlist utility. They may be searching music, using their own account libraries, importing local tracks, reading lyrics, or letting the visual stage react while music plays.

## Product Purpose

XK Radio combines music playback, lyrics, account playlists, local music, weather radio, particle visuals, wallpaper windows, and 3D stage effects into one Windows desktop experience. Success means users can get into music quickly while the interface feels cinematic, polished, and personal.

## Brand Personality

Private, cinematic, precise. The interface should feel like a quiet visual radio booth: dark, sharp, responsive, and music-driven.

## Anti-references

Do not make it look like a generic education SaaS dashboard, a card-heavy landing page, or a loud cyberpunk skin. Avoid large rounded cards, default browser controls, excessive accent colors, decorative blocks, and motion that feels busy or toy-like.

## Design Principles

- Keep the music stage first.
- Let motion feel like signal, playback, and state rather than decoration.
- Use dark surfaces with restrained electric-blue, cyan, champagne, and small warm highlights.
- Prefer crisp, compact controls with clear hover, active, and focus feedback.
- Preserve the current app shell and user workflow when polishing visual moments.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Support reduced-motion users with calm crossfades or static light fields. Preserve readable contrast on dark backgrounds, avoid color-only state communication, and keep interaction timing short enough that the product still feels responsive.
